Laboratory General Safety Rules and Policies  
====

 

1. Observe and abide all warning signs and safety notices.

2. Only students with toes-covered shoes are permitted to work in the lab.

3. Keep our workplace tidy at all times.

4. Report all the unsafe acts, unsafe conditions and injuries to the lab staffs.

5. Do not use non-approved plugs that is without “Safety mark” on it.

6. Use the correct tool for the hardware practical.

7. Upon hearing fire alarm, please follow the fire warden/lab staff’s instructions to evacuate to the nearest assembly point 40.

8. Do not obstruct passageway, exits or emergency fire-fighting equipment.

9. Switch off electrical supply when equipment not in use or after using.

10. More details on lab safety notices are placed available near its entrance.
